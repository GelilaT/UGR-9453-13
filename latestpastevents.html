Culture fest 2022

The festival showcased artistic and cultural values of the East African people. Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Dina Mufti said the festival played a significant role in promoting social, economic and political integration among the East African peoples. The festival was held for six consecutive days exhibiting indigenous manufacturing products, traditional cuisines and medicines, artistic pictures and sculptures, including languages and cultural symposium and book fairs. The Ethiopian National Theatre, Entoto Park and Mesqel square hosted the various programs.
